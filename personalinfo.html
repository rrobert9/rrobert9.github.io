Spirit Revived Band

I am the leader and rhythm guitarist for the Spirit Revived Band; a southern Christian rock group based out of Dallas, Ga. I started the band in March 2014 after leaving a previous southern gospel group. Currently we are working on recording and self-producing our first original album. Check us out on Facebook as well as Reverb Nation.


Volkswagen Enthusiast

I enjoy working on and daily-driving older water-cooled Volkswagens. I currently drive a 1991 Cabriolet that I have been slowly restoring over the past 5 - 6 years. Every year in mid-May I make a trip to Helen, Ga for the annual Southern Worthersee car show which is modeled after the orginal show that is held in Austria every year called Worthersee.

Video coverage this year's Southern Worthersee by BsaintMedia.

The build thread of my car can be found on Vwvortex.
